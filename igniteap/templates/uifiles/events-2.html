Pre-IGNITE

Pre-Ignite is a two-day event to enhance students' awareness of entrepreneurship and the startup ecosystem. The event serves as a platform for knowledge and team selection for the zonal-level competition.

Requirements Needed

Ensure that the participating college can provide a significant number of students to create a dynamic and engaging event.

The college should make available a suitable seminar hall equipped with Audio-visual and presentation equipment. This will facilitate a seamless flow of the event and enhance the learning experience.

Provide comfortable lodging and meals for our respected mentors and judges to ensure their participation and engagement in the event.

The college should offer mementoes to our esteemed judges and participating teams as a token of appreciation and recognition. This gesture reinforces the value of their contributions.

Collaborate with our team for smooth registration and coordination processes before and during the event.

Support event promotion within the college to maximize participation and ensure the event's success.
